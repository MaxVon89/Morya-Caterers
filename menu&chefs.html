Chefs

Our Professional Chefs

Madhuri Lokhande & Pallavi Khade

Head Chefs

Together, Madhuri Lokhande and Pallavi Khade are the backbone of मोरया Caterers. Madhuri Lokhande, renowned for her Puran Poli, has such a reputation that clients request its delivery across India. While her expertise lies in Maharashtrian cuisine, she is equally proficient in North and South Indian cuisine.

Pallavi Khade, the master of Biryani and non-vegetarian dishes, brings an unmatched flavor to every plate. With exceptional skills in non-vegetarian cooking, her dishes are loved by all.

Both chefs are experts in North and South Indian, as well as Chinese cuisine, and bring their collective experience to cater to a wide range of taste preferences.
